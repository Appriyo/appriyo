# Security Policy

## 🔒 Overview

Appriyo takes the security of its systems, website, and users seriously.
We appreciate the efforts of security researchers and the community in helping us maintain a safe and secure platform.

---

## 📢 Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please report it responsibly.

📧 **Email:** [contact@appriyo.com](mailto:contact@appriyo.com)

Please include the following details in your report:

* Description of the vulnerability
* Steps to reproduce the issue
* Potential impact
* Screenshots or proof-of-concept (if applicable)

---

## ⚠️ Responsible Disclosure

We kindly ask that you:

* Do NOT publicly disclose the vulnerability before it has been reviewed and resolved
* Avoid accessing or modifying user data
* Avoid disrupting services or degrading performance

We are committed to addressing valid security issues as quickly as possible.

---

## ⏱️ Response Time

* Initial response: Within 2–5 business days
* Resolution timeline: Depends on severity and complexity

---

## 🛡️ Supported Versions

Currently, only the **latest version** of this project is actively maintained and supported with security updates.

---

## 🚫 Out of Scope

The following are generally considered out of scope:

* UI/UX issues without security impact
* Performance improvements
* Non-exploitable bugs
* Issues in third-party dependencies (should be reported to the respective maintainers)

---

## 🤝 Acknowledgements

We appreciate responsible disclosures and may acknowledge contributors who help improve the security of our systems.

---

## 📌 Final Note

Security is a shared responsibility. By reporting vulnerabilities responsibly, you help us protect our users and maintain trust.

Thank you for your support.

— Appriyo Team
