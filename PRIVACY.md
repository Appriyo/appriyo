## 7. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Our measures include:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between your browser and our website is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
- **Access Controls:** Data access is restricted to team members who need it to perform their work
- **Authentication:** Strong authentication practices for internal systems
- **Minimal Data Storage:** We do not retain data beyond what is needed
- **Regular Reviews:** Periodic review of our security practices

No system is 100% secure. If you suspect a security incident involving your data, please contact us immediately at **contact@appriyo.com**.
